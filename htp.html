Fallschirmjäger

07-07-2024

The Russian Federation has invaded Germany with an experimental bio-weapon known as the Z Battalion. The soldiers of the Z Battalion are the zombified corpses of Russian civilians, soldiers and even criminals that are being sent to war in Germany as cannon fodder. You are a Fallschirmjäger, and you have found one of the barracks where the zombies are coming out from, your objective is to destroy the barracks before the zombies kill you.

How to play:

Kill zombies to earn cash to use buy more guns. Destroy the Z Battalion Barracks to win!

Controls:

Use W & S to move up and down, use 1, 2 & 3 to cycle between different guns, press [SPACE] to shoot.
